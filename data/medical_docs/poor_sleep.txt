Overview:
Poor sleep affects cognitive performance, mood, and overall health. It may be linked to lifestyle habits or underlying stress.

Causes:
- Irregular sleep schedule
- Excess screen time before bed
- Caffeine late in the day
- Noise or light exposure
- Stress or anxiety

Symptoms:
- Difficulty falling asleep
- Waking frequently at night
- Daytime sleepiness
- Irritability
- Poor concentration

Self-Care:
- Maintain a consistent sleep schedule.
- Avoid screens 1 hour before bed.
- Create a cool, dark sleep environment.
- Avoid heavy meals late at night.

Red Flags:
- Loud snoring + choking sensations (may indicate sleep apnea)
- Chronic insomnia (more than 3 months)
