Overview:
Anemia is a condition where the body lacks enough healthy red blood cells to carry oxygen effectively.

Common Causes:
- Iron deficiency
- Poor diet
- Heavy menstrual bleeding
- Chronic illness

Symptoms:
- Fatigue and weakness
- Pale skin
- Dizziness
- Headache
- Cold hands and feet
- Breathlessness during activity

Self-Care:
- Eat iron-rich foods (spinach, lentils, beans)
- Pair iron foods with vitamin C (helps absorption)
- Improve sleep habits
- Stay hydrated

Red Flags:
- Fainting
- Heart palpitations
- Extremely low stamina
