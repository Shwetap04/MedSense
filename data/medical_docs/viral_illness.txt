Overview:
Common viral infections cause mild, short-term symptoms and usually resolve without medication.

Symptoms:
- Fever
- Sore throat
- Runny nose
- Fatigue
- Body aches
- Mild cough

Self-Care:
- Rest and hydrate well
- Warm soups and herbal teas
- Saltwater gargles
- Steam inhalation

Red Flags:
- High fever for >3 days
- Difficulty breathing
- Persistent chest pain
