Overview:
Indigestion occurs when stomach acid irritates the stomach or esophagus. It is often linked to diet and lifestyle.

Common Causes:
- Spicy, oily, or fried foods
- Eating too fast or too late
- Caffeine, alcohol, soda
- Stress and anxiety
- Lying down immediately after eating

Symptoms:
- Burning sensation in chest/upper stomach
- Bloating
- Burping
- Nausea
- Sour taste in mouth

Self-Care:
- Eat smaller, lighter meals
- Avoid lying down for 2 hours after eating
- Reduce spicy/greasy foods
- Drink more water
- Try ginger or warm water for relief

Red Flags:
- Chest pain radiating to arm/jaw
- Persistent vomiting
- Blood in stool/vomit
