Overview:
Allergies occur when the immune system reacts to everyday substances like dust, pollen, or certain foods.

Common Triggers:
- Dust mites
- Pollen
- Pet dander
- Mold
- Cold weather
- Certain foods (nuts, eggs, milk)
- Insect bites

Symptoms:
- Sneezing
- Itchy/watery eyes
- Runny nose
- Skin rashes
- Coughing
- Mild wheezing

Self-Care:
- Keep rooms dust-free and well-ventilated
- Use a mask during high-pollen seasons
- Avoid known triggers
- Shower after going outdoors

Red Flags:
- Swelling of lips/tongue
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe rashes
