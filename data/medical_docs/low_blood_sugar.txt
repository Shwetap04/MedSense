Overview:
Low blood sugar can happen if you skip meals, exercise excessively, or consume too much caffeine.

Common Causes:
- Skipping meals
- Excessive exercise without eating
- Drinking coffee on an empty stomach
- Some medications (rare)

Symptoms:
- Shaking or weakness
- Dizziness
- Headache
- Sweating
- Fatigue
- Irritability

Self-Care:
- Eat a banana, glucose biscuit, or fruit juice.
- Rest for 15 minutes.
- Avoid long gaps between meals.

Red Flags:
- Confusion
- Fainting
- Seizures (rare)
