Title: Eye Strain (Digital Eye Fatigue)

Causes:
- Long screen time
- Poor lighting
- Reading too close

Symptoms:
- Burning sensation
- Watery eyes
- Headache
- Blurred vision

Self-Care:
- Follow 20-20-20 rule
- Reduce screen brightness
- Use artificial tears

Red Flags:
- Sudden vision loss
- Severe eye pain
