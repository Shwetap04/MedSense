Title: Muscle Strain – Overuse, Injury, and Recovery

Overview:
A muscle strain occurs when muscle fibers stretch or tear due to overuse, improper lifting, or sudden movement.

Common Causes:
- Lifting heavy objects incorrectly
- Sudden twisting or bending
- Sports injuries
- Repetitive movements
- Lack of warm-up

Symptoms:
- Sharp pain or soreness
- Swelling or stiffness
- Difficulty moving the affected area
- Muscle weakness

Self-Care (RICE Method):
- Rest for 24–48 hours
- Ice the area 15–20 mins
- Compression if swelling
- Elevate if possible

Additional Care:
- Gentle stretching after 48 hours
- Avoid heavy lifting for a few days
- Stay hydrated

Red Flags:
- Inability to move the limb
- Severe swelling/bruising
- Persistent pain beyond 1 week
