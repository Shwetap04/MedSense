Overview:
Motion sickness occurs when the brain receives mismatched signals from the eyes and inner ears.

Triggers:
- Car rides
- Boats
- Roller coasters
- VR experiences

Symptoms:
- Nausea
- Vomiting
- Dizziness
- Sweating
- Fatigue

Self-Care:
- Sit near a window and look outside.
- Avoid reading or screens during travel.
- Keep the vehicle well-ventilated.
- Eat light meals before traveling.

Red Flags:
- Persistent vomiting
- Severe dehydration
