Title: Stomach Infection (Gastroenteritis)

Overview:
Usually caused by contaminated food or viral infection.

Symptoms:
- Vomiting
- Watery diarrhea
- Abdominal cramps
- Mild fever
- Dehydration

Self-Care:
- Drink ORS frequently
- Eat soft, bland foods (bananas, rice, toast)
- Avoid dairy, oily foods, and caffeine

Red Flags:
- Blood in stool
- Severe dehydration
- Vomiting for more than 24 hours
